Welcome to Nonogram Game

Created By: Michael Chang

This puzzle is made up of a 15 by 15 table containing numbers are given at the top (and left side) of the table to indicate the number of unbroken “marked” lines in each column (and row).

The player’s job is to solve the puzzle by filling the cells of the table accordingly.

Once you complete the puzzle please click on "Submit Your Solution" button to see if you got the answer right.
